Start the week off right

Time

Level:Easy

Prep: 10 Min

Yeild: 4 Hamburgers


Total: 25 min

Cook: 15 min

Bengal Burger

26 Reviews

"No more dry, lackluster burgers. These are juicy, and spices can be easily added or changed to suit anyone's taste/ baste frequently with your favorite barbeque sauce. If you find the meat mixture too mushy, just add more bread crumbs until it forms patties that hold their shape."

Ingredients

  • 24 oz Ground Chuck
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Spice mix, recipe follows
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 slices of cheese, such as chedder, american cheese
  • 4 soft hamburger style buns, split
  • 4 slices beefsteak tomatoes, optional
  • Assorted lettcuies and greens,optional
  • Assorted mustard,optional
  • Mayonnaise,optional
  • Ketchup, optional

Spice Mix

  • 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • Hot Sauce, to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F. Place a roasting rack on a foil-lined baking sheet in the oven.
  2. Use your hands, break the meat into small pieces and combine evenly but loosely on a parchment or waxed paper-lined baking sheet. Spread the meat out and season it generously with salt and pepper. If desired, add the spice mix at this time.
  3. Divide the meat into 4 portions(about 6 ounces each). Using yur hands, form each portion into a ball-shape by gently tossing it from one hand to the ther. (Dont overwork or press too firmly on the meat.) Gently form each portion into a patty about 3 1/2 inches wide and 1-inch-thick.
  4. Preheat a large cast iron skilled over medium-low heat for 5 minutes. Raise the heat to high and add the oil. Add the patties and cook, turning once, until well-browned about 2 minutes each side.
  5. Using a spatula, transfer the hamburgers to the roasting rack in the oven and continue cking to desired doneness, 8 to 9 minutes fr medium rare, 10-11 minutes for medium, 13-15 minutes for well done. If you are using cheese, top the hamburgers during their last couple minutes of cooking to melt.
  6. Transfer the hamburgers to a plate, let rest for a couple minutes before serving. Meanwhile, toast the hamburger buns. Assemble the hamburgers with the condiments and toppings of your chouce. Serce
  7. When cooking on a countertop grill, turn on grill, then place on the heated grill.

    8. Timing for a countertop grill:

  8. For Medium-Rare: Cook for 3 minutes, covered and then unplug the machine, and continue to cook covered for 2 to 3 more minutes.
  9. For Medium: Cook for 4 minutes covered and then unplug the machine and continue to cook covered for 4 more minutes.
  10. For Well: Cook for 5 minites covered and then unplug the machine
